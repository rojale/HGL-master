Points and Prizes!

Hearthstone Gaming League hosts tournaments and leagues that reward the winners with HGL points. HGL points can be acquired through top finishes in any of our tournaments or leagues and even giveaways. HGL points can then be used to win cool prizes from Hearthstone packs and merchandise, to tons of other cool gaming gear!


What Are HGL Points?

HGL Points are points that you can earn through playing in our tournaments and leagues. How many HGL Points you get in these tournaments and leagues is determined by how well you do. Once you have acquired HGL Points you can see how many you have and what your competitors have earned through our Standings Page . Once you have earned HGL Points you can then put them to use by exchanging them for prizes such as hearthstone packs, gift cards, and much more. To find out more about our prizes and what you can chooses from you can go to our Prizes Page. Now that you know all about HGL Points and what you can win from them go join one of our Daily Tournaments !
